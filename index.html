About Me

Get to know more about my background and what drives me

I studied Fashion Design and Management because I’ve always been a creative and curious person, passionate about aesthetics and storytelling through visuals. After completing a Master’s in Digital Marketing, I discovered a growing interest in how things work behind the screen. That curiosity led me to start studying Full Stack Development — I wanted to combine creativity with logic and build things from scratch that people can interact with. Beyond design and code, I’m really into fitness and spending time in nature — I believe movement and fresh air are the best ways to recharge. I’m also drawn to everything visual: photography, architecture, and spaces that inspire.
